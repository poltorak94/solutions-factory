  • Proactive updates for D365FO and related components

We specialize in implementing two core products of the Microsoft Dynamics 365 cloud platform:

  • Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations (D365FO)
  • Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement (D365CE)

These solutions provide a comprehensive framework for automating and managing business processes. D365FO focuses on financial and operational management, covering accounting, budgeting, procurement, logistics, warehouse operations, production, planning, and asset management. D365CE is designed to optimize customer interaction, including sales and relationship management, customer support, and marketing automation.

Both solutions seamlessly integrate with Microsoft tools like Office 365, Power BI, and the Power Platform, offering a unified approach to business management and customer engagement.

A key trend in Microsoft Dynamics 365 implementations is the use of Power Platform technology. This low-code solution enables integration, application creation, and data-driven reporting without the need for advanced programming skills. However, it’s important to consider certain limitations, such as high licensing costs and occasional performance issues

We have successfully automated processes for over 20 companies across industries including manufacturing, logistics, distribution, and service operations. Our team has in-depth expertise in standard D365FO and D365CE modules, ensuring efficient process automation with minimal customizations.
