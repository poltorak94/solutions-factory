Мigration to Cloud D365FO
Client Challenge
The client faced a key question: “Should we migrate to a cloud platform?” They sought to simplify IT management, enhance security, and reduce costs, but migrating an ERP system like Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations (D365FO) involved risks, particularly with data access and integration restructuring
Results
- Reduced IT costs and enhanced system security. - Improved scalability and flexibility with Azure’s ecosystem. - Seamless access to advanced Microsoft cloud services.
Why Us
With proven expertise in cloud migrations, we ensure a smooth transition tailored to your business needs.
Objective
Enable a seamless migration to Microsoft’s cloud platform, ensuring scalability, cost efficiency, and access to advanced Azure tools.
Our Approach
- Assessment: Reviewed the client’s infrastructure and identified migration requirements.- Migration Execution: Transferred data and optimized integrations for the cloud. Leveraged Azure tools like Power Platform and DevOps for scalability and reliability. Support: Provided training to ensure smooth adoption of the new system.
